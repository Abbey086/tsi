About TSI

OUR STORY

Founded on Purpose

Talent Search Initiative (TSI) was founded on September 3, 2025, in Kampala, Uganda, with a burning desire to bridge the gap between raw potential and professional success.

We believe that every young person possesses a unique gift that, if properly nurtured, can become a vehicle for both personal success and national development.

Our Vision

To build a Uganda where every talent is discovered, nurtured and transformed into a vehicle for personal and national development.

Our Mission

To discover, empower and promote talents across all sectors, fostering creativity, innovation and sustainable livelihoods for young people and community.
