Shinah Galandi

Ambassador for Culture

Galandi Shinah Daphine was born on 22nd/10/2008 at Uganda Martyrs Hospital Lubaga. Growing up, Shinah showed an early interest in fashion and design. She studied her nursery up to primary two at Mustard Seed Nursery and Primary School, thereafter went to St. Lawrence Junior School - Kabowa in primary three where she completed her Primary Leaving Examinations in 2020/21.

She joined Elite High School - Entebbe in 2022 and during this period in the year 2024, she joined the Teen Miss Tourism pageant where she emerged:

  • Teen Miss Tourism Personality 2024-2025
  • Teen Miss Tourism Best Cultural Wear 2024-2025
  • Teen Miss Tourism Best Creative Wear 2024-2025

During her reign, she attended the Uganda-China Symposium at Sheraton Hotel in Kampala City.

Racheal Kengiri

Ambassador for entertainment

Racheal Kengini was born on 3rd September 2008. From a young age, she demonstrated a strong passion and natural talent for dance and music, interests that have continued to shape her personal and creative journey. She began her education at Aunt Rose Primary School in Ntugamo District, where her talent and enthusiasm for the performing arts became evident.

Upon completing her primary education, Racheal joined Buyubuli Secondary School for her O-Level studies, further developing both her academic and artistic abilities. Racheal later advanced to Green Valley High School, where she is currently pursuing her A-Level education.Alongside her studies, she remains deeply committed to nurturing her talent in dance and music, consistently striving for growth and excellence.

Driven by determination and passion, Racheal aspires to achieve her dreams and make a meaningful impact through her artistic expression. Her journey reflects resilience, creativity, and a strong commitment to personal development.
