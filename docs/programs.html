PERFORMYX Dancers

A flagship dance and performance development program under the Talent Search Initiative that identifies, nurtures, and empowers young people aged 10-25 with exceptional dance talent. We transform movement into purpose through professional training, mentorship, and performance platforms, serving as a creative pathway for youth expression, discipline, leadership, and professional performance while preserving and innovating African and contemporary dance forms.
